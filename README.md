# Gods as Athletes

Gods as Athletes is a strategy-management game about cultivating rival universes and shaping the gods who compete for cosmic survival.

You guide divine competitors through seasons of preparation and automated clashes, deciding where each universe should focus before the next contest.

## Gameplay

- Allocate focus to shape divine strengths.
- Advance through season phases.
- Watch automated clashes decide outcomes.
- Compare rival universes and adapt your plans.
- Build toward long-term survival over multiple seasons.

## Goal

Create the strongest surviving cosmos by preparing gods well enough to win the clashes that matter.

## Controls

- Mouse: allocate focus and advance phases.
- Esc: back or pause.

## Current Scope

Playable strategy loop with universe management, divine stat shaping, automated contests, and multi-season pressure.
